True Cloud Orchestration Across Clouds

VMWare has been a leader of the enterprise virtualization space for many years and is by far the most popular industry solution to date. On top of their virtualization solutions, VMware has also entered the cloud arena with private and public cloud offerings - from vCloud through vCloudAir.

As a result, with VMware, there are often times the traditional vSphere virtualization integrations alongside vCloud integrations, not to mention other clouds from OpenStack deployments, through public clouds like EC2 and Softlayer.

That's why it was an important target for Cloudify to be able to expande its already broad support of cross-cloud orchestration, to support VMware's virtualization and cloud offerings. Through our VMware plugin, you receive common management and orchestration as an abstraction for your deployment infrastructre of both VMware and any other cloud. The application is kept aware of which infrastructure it is running on, and with all the calls to each of the infrastructure components centralized into one driver per environment, it is easily managed once for all the applications.
